Joshua Cader

PhD candidate in the Graduate School of Interdisciplinary Information Studies
at the University of Tokyo working under Professor Yasuhiro Matsuda.

Research interests
Social movements, internet culture, and international relations — in particular:

  • civic media
  • street protest tactics and framing
  • East Asian security
  • authoritarian resilience, democratic consolidation
  • determinants of internet community characteristics
  • social construction of violence

  • Laurel Hart, Pamela Lamb, and Joshua Cader, “Networked Technologies as Sites and Means of Nonviolence: Interdisciplinary Perspectives,” Girlhood Studies 10, no. 2 (Summer 2017): 62-79.
